Grannies Famous Chicken Noodle Soup

  • Time: 40 min
  • Serves: 8

Step 1. Place chicken broth and shredded chicken over low heat in a medium sauce pan Step 2. Add seasonings of your choice and mix Step 3. Let Soup thicken for 3 minutes and then enjoy with friends!

Ingredients Quantity Guaranteed Fullness
Shredded Chicken Breast 2 cups 100%
Chicken Broth 3 cups
Seasonigs of your choice teaspoon each

Lar's Ube Cake

Source: Ube Frosting(if your feeling extra)

Step 1. Cream butter and sugar together in a stand mixer for 6 minutes
Step 2. Slowly add eggs 1 by 1 until fully mixed in
Step 3. Add ube extract
Step 4. Add flour then milk silmultaneously until fully mixed in
Ingredients Quantity Guaranteed Fullness
Ube Extract teaspoon 45% but worth it
Cake flour 2 cups
Sugar 2 cups
milk 1/2 cup
Unsalted Butter(softened) 2 sticks
eggs 3
